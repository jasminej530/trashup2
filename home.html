Contributions to the Environment

Plastic Bags Instead of using plastic bags when going grocery shopping use reusable bags to help the environment more. These bags can be made of paper or fabric (tweed, cotton, etc).
Plastic Water Bottle Instead of using plastic water bottles use reusable bottles such as a hydroflask or metal water bottle when drinking water. That way there isn't plastic building up and it would help the environment more. >
Plastic Straws Instead of using plastic straws when having a drink, use a reusable straw such as a metal or bamboo straw since they last longer and is safer for the environment.
Gas Powered Vehicle Instead of using your own personal car when going around use electrical car or public transportation so more people can use the vehicle at once and will help the environment more or bike or walk to more.
